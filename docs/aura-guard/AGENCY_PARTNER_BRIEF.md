# AURA-GUARD Agency Partner Brief

## Positioning
AURA-GUARD can be packaged by agencies and B2B consultants as an AI governance audit add-on.

The partner owns the client relationship. AURA-GUARD supplies the intake structure, scoring logic, dashboard flow, report generator, and executive report format.

## Partner Package Range
$5,000–$10,000 depending on customization, delivery support, and partner scope.

## Included Assets
- Audit intake questionnaire
- AI risk scoring model
- Dashboard workflow
- Report generator structure
- Printable executive report format
- Offer one-pagers
- Sales email sequence
- Client delivery checklist
- Partner Mode workspace structure

## Delivery Workflow
1. Client receives the AI Risk Audit offer.
2. Client completes intake.
3. Partner reviews AI usage, data exposure, vendor risk, and approval process.
4. Risk score is generated.
5. Executive report is prepared.
6. Client receives findings, risk drivers, and 30-day roadmap.
7. Partner can offer implementation support or monitoring.

- Keep the product premium.
- Do not sell it as a cheap template.
- Do not present it as legal certification.
