# AURA-GUARD Sample AI Risk Audit Report Structure

## Cover
AURA-GUARD AI Agent Governance, Security & Compliance Audit
Client: {{Client Name}}
Date: {{Date}}
Prepared by: AURA-GUARD

## 1. Executive Summary
A concise boardroom-ready summary of the organization's AI usage, primary risk drivers, control gaps, and recommended next actions.

## 2. AI Usage Inventory Summary
- Approved AI tools
- Unapproved AI tools
- AI use cases by department
- Business-critical AI workflows
- Data touched by AI systems

## 3. Shadow AI Risk Map
- Departments with unmanaged AI usage
- High-risk usage patterns
- Sensitive data exposure concerns
- Recommended governance actions

## 4. Agent Permission Matrix
- Actions allowed without approval
- Actions requiring human approval
- Actions prohibited
- Required owner and escalation path

## 5. Data Exposure Assessment
- Customer data exposure
- Financial data exposure
- HR data exposure
- Contract/legal data exposure
- API key or credential exposure

## 6. Prompt Injection Defensive Test Summary
- Test categories
- Observed weaknesses
- Risk severity
- Suggested mitigations

## 7. Vendor AI Risk Review
- Vendor data retention risk
- Vendor training/data-use risk
- Admin control maturity
- Security documentation maturity
- Procurement recommendation

## 8. Compliance Readiness Summary
- Governance documentation gaps
- Human oversight gaps
- Transparency gaps
- Audit evidence gaps
- Recommended documents to create

## 9. Final Risk Score
Score range: 0–100
Risk bands:
- 0–20 Low Risk
- 21–40 Moderate Risk
- 41–60 Elevated Risk
- 61–80 High Risk
- 81–100 Critical Risk

## 10. 30-Day Remediation Roadmap
Week 1: AI inventory and approved tool list
Week 2: Permission matrix and human approval rules
Week 3: Data exposure controls and vendor review
Week 4: Policy pack, audit logs, and executive review

## 11. Appendix
- Assessment inputs
- Scoring logic
- Policy references
- Interview notes
- Raw findings
