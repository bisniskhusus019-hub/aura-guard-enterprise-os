# AURA-GUARD Dashboard Page Map

## Overview
Shows total AI risk score, risk band, high-risk modules, open remediation tasks, latest reports, and AI inventory status.

## AI Inventory
Tracks approved and unapproved AI tools, owners, vendors, departments, use cases, and data touched.

## Shadow AI
Maps unofficial AI usage by department, risk level, and policy violation potential.

## Agents
Lists AI agents/workflows, autonomy level, production status, owner, connected tools, and data sources.

## Permissions
Displays allowed, approval-required, and prohibited actions per agent.

## Data Risk
Maps data sources touched by AI and sensitivity levels.

## Prompt Injection Tests
Stores defensive test results, severity, and mitigation recommendations.

## Compliance
Shows readiness gaps, required documents, internal policy status, and evidence tracking.

## Vendors
Reviews vendor AI tools by privacy, security, admin controls, data retention, and training risk.

## Policies
Stores generated AI policies: acceptable use, employee AI usage, vendor AI policy, human approval policy.

## Audit Logs
Shows agent actions, tool calls, risk events, and summary explanations.

## Reports
Generates and stores executive AI risk audit reports.

## White Label
Supports partner branding, client workspaces, and agency report packaging.

## Settings
Organization profile, user roles, plan, billing status, and integrations.
