# AURA-GUARD Audit Intake Form Fields

## Company Profile
- Company name
- Website
- Industry
- Country/region
- Company size
- Main buyer contact
- Contact email
- Department owner

## AI Usage
- Which AI tools are currently used?
- Who uses each tool?
- What business function uses AI?
- Is usage approved by management?
- Is there an internal AI policy?

## Data Exposure
- Does AI touch customer data?
- Does AI touch financial data?
- Does AI touch HR data?
- Does AI touch contracts/legal documents?
- Does AI access internal knowledge bases?
- Does AI access API keys, credentials, or technical logs?

## Agent Autonomy
- Does AI only draft responses?
- Can AI send emails?
- Can AI update CRM/database records?
- Can AI approve refunds/payments?
- Can AI modify files?
- Can AI call external tools/API?

## Human Approval
- Which AI actions need human approval?
- Who approves high-risk actions?
- Is there a written escalation process?
- Are approvals logged?

## Vendor Risk
- Which external AI vendors are used?
- Are privacy policies reviewed?
- Is data used for training?
- Is data deletion/export available?
- Are admin controls available?

## Security Testing
- Has the chatbot/agent been tested against prompt injection?
- Has data leakage testing been performed?
- Are outputs filtered before being sent externally?
- Are logs reviewed?

## Desired Output
- Starter Audit
- Professional Audit
- Enterprise Readiness Report
- White-Label Partner Review
