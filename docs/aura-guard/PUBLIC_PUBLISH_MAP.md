## Publish rule
Public website pages can be promoted before payment is connected. Payment-dependent buttons should remain checkout-ready but not advertised as live checkout until payment credentials and price IDs are configured.

## What publish means before payment
Before payment is connected, publish means:

- Buyers can read the offer
- Buyers can inspect pricing
- Buyers can submit intake/contact/demo forms after Supabase env is configured
- Dashboard can be shown as product demo
- Trust/legal pages are present
- Payment can stay disabled or setup-needed until final activation
