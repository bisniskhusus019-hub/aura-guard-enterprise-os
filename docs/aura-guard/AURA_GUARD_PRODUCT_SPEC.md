# AURA-GUARD — Enterprise AI Agent Governance, Security & Compliance OS

## Product Decision

AURA-GUARD is the primary product direction. It is built as a high-ticket B2B SaaS and audit platform for organizations adopting AI agents, AI tools, chatbots, and workflow automation.

## Core Promise

Audit, control, and secure company AI usage before AI agents leak data, break policy, or fail compliance expectations.

## Buyer Segments

### 1. AI Automation Agencies
Need to add governance evidence to client automation projects.

### 2. SaaS Startups
Need AI risk documentation before enterprise customers, investors, or partners ask.

### 3. SMBs Using AI Tools
Need internal AI policy, approved tool lists, data exposure controls, and human approval rules.

### 4. B2B Consultants
Can resell AI governance audits as a white-label service.

## Primary Revenue Engine

The first sellable product is not a toy MVP. The core revenue engine is the AI Risk Audit Report Generator.

The platform must support:

- AI usage inventory collection
- Risk score generation
- Agent permission mapping
- Prompt injection defensive test summary
- Data exposure mapping
- Vendor risk summary
- Compliance gap summary
- Executive PDF report generation
- 30-day remediation roadmap

## Risk Scoring Formula

Risk Score = Data Sensitivity + Tool Autonomy + External Exposure + Approval Weakness + Vendor Risk + Prompt Injection Risk + Logging Weakness + Compliance Gap.

### Score Bands

- 0–20: Low Risk
- 21–40: Moderate Risk
- 41–60: Elevated Risk
- 61–80: High Risk
- 81–100: Critical Risk

## Build Principle

Build enterprise architecture from day one. Do not build a small throwaway version. The first commercial output is the audit report engine, but the database, dashboard, and product structure must be ready for SaaS expansion.
