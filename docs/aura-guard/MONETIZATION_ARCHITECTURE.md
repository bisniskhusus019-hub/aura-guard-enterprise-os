# AURA-GUARD Monetization Architecture

## Goal
AURA-GUARD should not depend on one small purchase. The revenue model is designed as a ladder:

1. Paid audit entry
2. Professional audit
3. Enterprise readiness package
4. Monthly monitoring subscription
5. Enterprise OS subscription
6. Agency or strategic partner license

## Offer Ladder

| Tier | Price | Revenue Type | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Starter Audit | $299 | One-time | Entry product for fast risk baseline |
| Professional Audit | $999 | One-time | Main audit product for serious B2B buyers |
| Enterprise Readiness | $2,500 | One-time | Higher-ticket evidence package |
| Monitoring Core | $499/mo | Subscription | Recurring dashboard/report value |
| Monitoring Scale | $1,500/mo + $2,500 setup | Subscription | Multi-workspace monitoring |
| Enterprise OS | $5,000/mo + $10,000 setup | Subscription | Full AI governance OS |
| Agency Partner License | $7,500 | Partner | Agency resale package |
| Strategic Partner License | $15,000 + $5,000 setup | Partner | Larger partner package |

## Upsell Logic

### Starter Audit to Professional Audit
If the client has customer data exposure, Shadow AI, or AI tools used by multiple departments, recommend Professional Audit.

### Professional Audit to Enterprise Readiness
If the client has enterprise customers, procurement pressure, investor diligence, or AI agents with autonomy, recommend Enterprise Readiness.

### Enterprise Readiness to Monitoring
If the client wants continuous visibility, monthly reporting, or multiple workspaces, recommend Monitoring Core or Scale.

### Monitoring to Enterprise OS
If the client has many AI workflows, many teams, compliance evidence needs, or partner/client reporting needs, recommend Enterprise OS.

### Agency to Partner License
If the buyer is an AI automation agency or consultant, position AURA-GUARD as a reseller-ready governance audit system.

## Entitlement Logic
Each plan controls:

- number of reports
- number of workspaces
- number of AI tools
- partner mode access
- custom branding access
- compliance mapping access
- priority support access

## Important Rule
Do not present AURA-GUARD as a cheap template. The product should be positioned as risk visibility, governance evidence, executive reporting, and operational control for AI adoption.
